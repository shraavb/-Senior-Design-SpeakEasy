# Audio Recording Prompts - INTERMEDIATE Level (B1)
## From Movie Dialogues (Ground Truth Reference)

**Project:** SpeakEasy - Catalan Spanish Language Learning App  
**Purpose:** Collect learner audio samples with movie dialogue ground truth  
**Target Speakers:** Comfortable newbies + Good speakers  
**Estimated Time:** ~25 minutes

---

## Recording Instructions

1. **Read the context first** - Understand the movie situation
2. **Record both speakers** - Natural conversation flow
3. **Try to incorporate Spanish slang** where it feels natural

---

## Spanish Slang Reference

| Slang | Meaning |
|-------|---------|

| mola | it's cool |
