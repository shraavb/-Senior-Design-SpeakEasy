# Audio Recording Prompts - BEGINNER Level (A1-A2)
## From Movie Dialogues (Ground Truth Reference)

**Project:** SpeakEasy - Catalan Spanish Language Learning App  
**Purpose:** Collect learner audio samples with movie dialogue ground truth  
**Target Speakers:** All newbie speakers (4 people)  
**Estimated Time:** ~25 minutes

---

## Recording Instructions

1. **Read the context first** - Understand the situation from the movie
2. **Record both speakers** - You can do both parts yourself or with a partner
3. **Speak at your current level** - mistakes are valuable data!
4. **Pause briefly between speaker turns** (1-2 seconds)

---

## File Naming Format
```
{your_name}_{scenario}_{dialogue_number}.wav
```
Example: `john_greetings_01.wav`
